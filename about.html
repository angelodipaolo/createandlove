Fine Art & Design For Happy Living

My name is Carly Di Paolo.

I live and work in Pennsylvania, a land-locked, moderately-sized rectangle in the eastern United States.

My love for the natural world is the driving force behind the style of my work.  I am always searching for new ways to convey my delight and amazement at the majesty and mystery of nature. The sources of inspiration that mother nature provides are endless.  I find myself constantly interpreting and emulating her nuances and juxtapositions.   My artwork is a tribute to her soft curves and jagged edges, her vastness and her intricate details, her ebbs, flows and glorious interwoven circles of growth, decay and evolution.

My reason for creating art is simple:  I can’t help myself. 

It is not easy.  It is not cheap.  At times, it can be downright frustrating and exhausting.  Yet I press on, because I have found there is no other activity on this earth which has made me feel as content and most myself as I feel when I am being an artist.  And while it is no secret that a career in the arts is a narrow and difficult path, I continue to feel driven to hone my craft, to keep learning, to try harder.  I find more with every day that being a full-time artist, however challenging, is the only life I can ever pursue in the hopes of true happiness

I am at my best when I am working with my hands, creating something unique, and moving forward knowing that the world is a little more interesting with another artwork in it.

I hope my art will make your world a little more interesting, too.
